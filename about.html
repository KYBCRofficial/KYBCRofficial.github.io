About KYBCR Exchange

Corporate Profile

KYBCR Exchange (Kyberon Core Group) is a financial technology infrastructure organization headquartered in San Francisco, specializing in institutional-grade digital asset systems.

The company develops trading engines, liquidity frameworks, custody systems, and settlement infrastructure designed for global financial institutions.

KYBCR is positioned as a financial infrastructure provider rather than a retail exchange, focusing on backend systems powering digital asset markets.

Headquarters

Team Structure

Engineering Division • Compliance Division • Risk Control Division • Global Operations Division

Vision & Mission

Our mission is to build a global digital financial infrastructure that connects traditional financial systems with blockchain-based markets.

We aim to create a transparent, secure, and scalable ecosystem where institutional participants can operate with confidence and efficiency.
